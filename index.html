About Me

I'm a film director and virtual production operator based in Madrid. I direct films and build the VFX workflows that make them, so I'm either on set or at my desk solving a shot.


I learned virtual production from scratch and worked on international stages for Netflix, ITV and others. Now I'm building AI tools on top of it: white-wall compositing and background generation, mostly. That work got me speaking at Epic Games and a few VP gatherings.


My main project is Low Power Mode, a story about a tiny civilisation living inside a phone. I've wanted to make it for years. Most of what I build is really just me trying to get it made.
