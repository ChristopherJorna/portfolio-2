About Me

I've wanted to tell one story since I was a kid.


I used to sit on the stairs with my dad's earphones in, listening to the Black Eyed Peas, sure there were little people inside the cable making the music. That idea never left me. It grew into Low Power Mode, a story about a tiny civilisation living inside a phone, and I made the first version as my masters film.


To make it I had to learn virtual production from scratch. That became a career of its own: working on international stages for Netflix and ITV, developing AI tools like white-wall compositing, and speaking about the work at Epic Games and other VP gatherings.


I love the technical side, but the story is why I do any of it. I want to make films that warm your heart, make you smile, and get you moving. I'm just getting started.
